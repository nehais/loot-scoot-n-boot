A cargo ship has wrecked, leaving its valuable goods scattered across the sea. Pirates are on the loose, looting them. You’ve been deployed on an armed ship with one mission: recover the target cargos before the sun sets. The race is on against time and the pirates.
Will you succeed?

Game Instructions

Use the arrow keys to navigate your ship.

Land on cargo to collect it.

The number on the cargo indicates its weight.

When halfway to the target, your ship's speed will decrease.

Colliding with a pirate ship has Collision Damage

Press the spacebar to shoot down pirate ships.

Shooting down a pirate ship will reward you with a HUGE cargo.
